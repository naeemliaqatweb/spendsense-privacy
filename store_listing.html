Full Description (Oppo App Store & Google Play Console)
SpendSense is the ultimate premium budget manager and AI grocery assistant designed to help you track spending, budget smarter, and save on every store run. Equipped with a powerful offline scanner and dynamic AI analytics, SpendSense helps you instantly organize your grocery receipts, track price histories, and optimize your monthly budget—all while keeping your financial data 100% private. KEY FEATURES: 📷 SMART RECEIPT SCANNING * Instantly scan and read grocery receipts using your device camera. * Automatically parses item names, quantities, individual item costs, and total billing. * Organizes receipts by date, store, and category for easy review. 💰 PERSONALIZED BUDGET PLANNING * Set dynamic daily, weekly, or monthly spending limits. * View clean visual progress indicators showing exactly how much budget remains. * Restrict items or set smart goals to prevent overspending. 🤖 REAL-TIME AI SPENDING INSIGHTS * Powered by Gemini, get instant advice on your spending patterns. * Tailored savings recommendations based on your local area and country. * Localized suggestions adapt automatically if you are shopping inside Pakistan or abroad (e.g. Dubai, UAE). 📊 ADVANCED ANALYTICS & BREAKDOWNS * Beautiful interactive charts showing categories and items. * Group expenditures by store (e.g. Carrefour, Lulu, Metro, local marts). * View daily and monthly trend graphs to identify where your money goes. 🔒 100% PRIVATE & OFFLINE-FIRST * Your data never leaves your phone. SpendSense stores all receipts locally inside secure sandbox storage. * Fast, lightweight, and works fully offline. Download SpendSense today and take complete control of your shopping budget!
