SpendSense Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2026. Your privacy is under your absolute control.

SpendSense is built with a privacy-first architecture. All your shopping lists, item details, monthly budgets, and spending metrics are stored locally on your device. We do not operate external databases, run analytics servers, or track your behavior.

100% Local Storage

All scanned grocery items, category structures, receipt logs, and monthly spending profiles are saved completely inside your device's isolated application storage. No external hosting holds your data.

Gemini Chat AI Safety

Conversations with the AI budget assistant are kept offline on your device. Chat payloads are sent securely to Google Gemini APIs only when submitting queries and are never logged or stored by us.

On-Device Receipt Scanning

Invoice text reading is processed on-device. The text matches are temporarily formatted via Google Gemini APIs to populate your lists. Captured receipt images are deleted immediately after scanning.

Data Backup & Ownership

You maintain complete ownership of your historical metrics. You can export a JSON backup file to your own storage at any time and import it to restore your profile on a new device.

Secure Local API Keys

Your personal Google Gemini API Key is stored inside encrypted local AsyncStorage. It is used solely for querying Gemini services straight from your device and is never uploaded.

Complete Reset Control

You have the right to be forgotten. You can completely erase all scanned receipts, budget history, custom categories, location tags, and AI insights from your device instantly at any time.

Information Collection & Use

We do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) or financial details. We request permissions on your device solely for local capabilities:

  • Camera: Used to capture receipts for instant receipt text reading.
  • Location: Used locally to detect country and city contexts for location-relevant grocery tips.

Third-Party Services

To provide advanced assistant features and display advertisement support, the app integrates the following third-party libraries:

  • Google AdMob: Integrated for ad banner display. Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and location data for ad delivery.
  • Google Gemini API: Integrated to format parsed invoices and power the offline AI budget assistant. Google Gemini handles query data according to their AI safety frameworks.

Children's Privacy

SpendSense does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out via our GitHub repository issues or contact us.
