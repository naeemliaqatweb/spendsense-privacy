How It Works

Everything You Need to Save Smart

100% Local Privacy

All grocery lists, scanned receipts, monthly budgets, and spending metrics stay safely stored in your device's local memory. No cloud accounts required.

On-Device Receipt Scanning

Simply snap a picture of your invoice! The app automatically extracts item names, quantities, and prices to immediately update your spend logs.

Gemini AI Assistant

Have questions about commodity pricing or savings? Chat directly with an integrated AI budget assistant that adjusts details based on your city.

Interactive Analytics

Visualize spending metrics. Explore daily, weekly, and monthly category breakdowns, progress meters, and AI savings insights in dark or light mode.

Active Budget Caps

Set custom budget limits. Receive dynamic visual indicators (green to red progress alerts) inside your spending card as you approach your threshold.

Full Backup Ownership

Maintain absolute control of your data. Export a complete JSON backup to your local storage or import it to sync settings onto a new mobile device.

Behind the Code

Built with Passion & Privacy in Mind

SpendSense is designed and developed by Naeem Liaqat as a modern, premium utility app to help individuals and families handle financial budgeting without giving up their data privacy.

Using advanced on-device parsing APIs and secure integrations with Google Gemini models, the app delivers a cutting-edge assistant experience while keeping 100% of sensitive information securely stored in local offline sandbox containers on your Android device.
